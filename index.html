A Tribute To Piyush Pandey

Redefining The Craft Of Indian AdWorld
The man himself on the cover of his book Pandeymonium

Piyush Pandey is a creative legend who redefined every aspect of Indian advertisement industry with his genuinely out_of_TheBox thought process.. In the History of Modern Independent India, his name will be commemorated with great honor. He is More than just a man who helped businesses to brand and rebrand. He showed the world, how power of art can inspire and change the life of billions. The Polio eradication campaign in India under his creative skills have proven to be one of the most successful social campaigns held in any country. As a result India became, a 100% Polio free nation. In this tribute page, I'm listing down some of his works which are my personal favourites----> Enjoy!
